Safety Philosophy

Every behavioral spec includes explicit refusal boundaries, not just capabilities. My agents enforce "fail closed on ambiguity" rather than defaulting to helpfulness when the safe response is unclear. I coordinate defense-in-depth across agent teams because single-layer guardrails break. I build evaluation frameworks that test for harmful outputs, not just correct ones, because a prompt that produces good answers 99% of the time and dangerous answers 1% of the time is a dangerous prompt.
